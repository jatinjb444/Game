What is Cartesian Product?

The Cartesian product of two sets A and B, denoted as A × B, is the set of all possible ordered pairs (a, b) such that a is an element of A and b is an element of B.

For example, if:

A = {1, 2}, B = {x, y}, then:

A × B = {(1, x), (1, y), (2, x), (2, y)}

The Cartesian product is used in various fields such as mathematics, computer science, and logic.
